# Introduction to Crawl Pilot Extension

The web is a vast ocean of information, but extracting that information in a format you can actually use has always been a challenge. Whether you're a developer building a price comparison tool, a researcher gathering sentiment data, or a business analyst tracking competitors, you need a way to turn visual web pages into structured data.

That's where the **Crawl Pilot Extension** comes in.

## What is Crawl Pilot Extension?

The Crawl Pilot Extension is a powerful browser tool that brings the logic of our AI-powered scraping infrastructure directly to your browsing experience. It's designed to make data extraction as simple as clicking a button—no coding, no complex CSS selectors, and no brittle scripts required.

### Key Benefits

1.  **Visual Selection**: Point and click on the elements you want to extract. Our AI understands the context and finds similar items automatically across pages.
2.  **Instant Preview**: See exactly what your data will look like in real-time.
3.  **Local Processing**: Just like the rest of the CrawlPilot ecosystem, the extension prioritizes your privacy. Your extraction logic runs right in your browser.
4.  **Export Options**: One-click export to JSON, CSV, or direct integration with your existing CrawlPilot workflows.

## How to Get Started

Using the extension is a breeze:

1.  **Install & Pin**: Add the extension to your browser and pin it for quick access.
2.  **Navigate**: Go to any website you want to extract data from.
3.  **Select**: Open the extension and use the picker tool to select your target data points (e.g., product names, prices, reviews).
4.  **Extract**: Click 'Generate' and watch the AI structure the data for you.

## Why Use the Extension Instead of a Script?

While our CLI and API are great for large-scale automation, the extension is perfect for **discovery and prototyping**. It allows you to quickly validate if a site's data is extractable and helps you build the initial schema that can later be used in your automated workflows.

## Conclusion

The Crawl Pilot Extension is your gateway to a more open and accessible web. By removing the technical barriers to web scraping, we're empowering everyone to harness the power of web data.
