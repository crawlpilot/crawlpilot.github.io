The web is a vast ocean of information, but extracting that information in a format you can actually use has always been a challenge. Whether you're a developer building a price comparison tool, a researcher gathering sentiment data, or a business analyst tracking competitors, you need a way to turn visual web pages into structured data.

That's where the **Crawl Pilot Extension** comes in.

## What is Crawl Pilot Extension?

The Crawl Pilot Extension is a powerful browser tool that brings the logic of our AI-powered scraping infrastructure directly to your browsing experience. It's designed to make data extraction as simple as clicking a button—no coding, no complex CSS selectors, and no brittle scripts required. It operates 100% locally in your browser, ensuring your data never touches our servers.

### Key Benefits

1.  **Automated List Extraction**: Simply select an item card, and our AI automatically identifies the list pattern across the page.
2.  **Attribute Unification**: No manual mapping required. CrawlPilot automatically extracts data points (like price, title, and rank) from each card and unifies them into clean columns.
3.  **Local-First Architecture**: Your extraction logic, configurations, and data are stored locally using IndexedDB. Privacy is a feature, not an afterthought.
4.  **Integrated Data Tools**: Merge columns, filter results, and clean your data directly in the extension sidebar before exporting.

## How to Get Started

Using the extension is a breeze:

1.  **Install & Pin**: Add the extension to your browser and pin it for quick access.
2.  **Navigate**: Go to any website you want to extract data from.
3.  **Identify Card**: Open the extension and click a single "Product Card" or list item.
4.  **Extract & Refine**: Watch as the AI automatically populates the data table. Use the sidebar tools to filter or merge columns as needed.
5.  **Export**: Download your structured data as CSV or JSON instantly.

## Why Use the Extension?

CrawlPilot is perfect for **semantic discovery**. It allows you to quickly validate if a site's data is extractable and helps you build structured datasets without the overhead of writing scrapers. Because it understands the *meaning* of the content, your extraction is resilient to minor layout changes.

## Conclusion

The Crawl Pilot Extension is your gateway to a more open and accessible web. By removing the technical barriers to web scraping, we're empowering everyone to harness the power of web data.
